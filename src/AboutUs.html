About Us

Welcome to N&D, where every cup tells a story. Nestled in the heart of Colombo, our cozy café is dedicated to serving the finest quality coffee, sourced from sustainable farms around the globe.

At N&D, we believe that great coffee starts with great beans. That’s why we work directly with farmers who share our passion for quality and sustainability, ensuring that every cup we serve is not only delicious but also ethically sourced.

Our skilled baristas craft each beverage with care, whether it’s a bold espresso, a velvety latte, or a refreshing cold brew. We’re passionate about creating a warm, inviting atmosphere where people can gather, unwind, and enjoy the rich flavors of our brews.

But it’s not just about the coffee. Our menu also offers a variety of freshly baked pastries, snacks, and light bites to complement your drink of choice. Whether you’re here to catch up with friends, get some work done, or just take a break from the hustle and bustle, N&D is your perfect escape.

Thank you for making us a part of your day. We look forward to serving you and sharing our love for coffee, one cup at a time.
